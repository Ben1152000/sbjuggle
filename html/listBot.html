Automated Mailing List

If you want to come out and juggle with us, it's a good idea to join the mailing list. Sometimes we juggle at different locations or times. Sometimes nobody will show up at all! If there is a change in the meeting time or location, then a message is sent out to the e-mail list. You can easily unsubscribe at any time, and your e-mail address won't be distributed to anyone else.

To join the list, click here and enter your e-mail address.

To unsubscribe from the list, and send the e-mail that is created.
If a "click here" link is not displayed above, then unsubscribe by sending an e-mail to "SBJA+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com".
More information on unsubscribing from the list is available here .

You don't need to join the list to read the current list of messages .

The e-mail list is sponsored by Google. Feel free to check them out to assure yourself that they are legitimate. You may especially be interested in their privacy policy .

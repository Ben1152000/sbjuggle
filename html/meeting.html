Where and When

WEEKEND MEETING:

We usually meet every Saturday from 3:00pm - 6:00pm (or dark).

In these pandemic times we normally meet at Oak Park .

In the before (pandemic) times, during the UCSB school year, we would meet in Anisq'oyo Park, "downtown" Isla Vista.
We'll likely return to Isla Vista when the students and grass return.

ALTERNATE LOCATIONS:
Sometimes, especially during the summer, we meet at alternate locations in order to partake in local happenings. Click here for the calendar.
